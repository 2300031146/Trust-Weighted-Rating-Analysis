# Trust-Weighted Rating Index

## Project Overview

This project develops a Trust-Weighted Rating Index to evaluate product reliability more accurately than traditional rating metrics. Instead of relying solely on star ratings, the analysis incorporates review volume and return behavior to measure customer trust and purchase confidence.

## Objective

To calculate a Trust-Weighted Average Rating and compare it with:

* Simple Average Rating
* Review-Weighted Rating

The goal is to identify whether highly rated products are genuinely trustworthy when customer engagement and return patterns are considered.

## Trust Score Formula

Trust Score = Rating × LOG(1 + Review Count) × (1 − Return Rate)

## Key Measures Created

* Trust Score
* Trust-Weighted Rating
* Review-Weighted Rating
* Simple Average Rating

## Dashboard Components

* KPI Card: Simple Average Rating
* KPI Card: Review-Weighted Rating
* KPI Card: Trust-Weighted Rating
* Comparison Bar Chart
* Trust Score Distribution Analysis
* Product-Level Trust Evaluation

## Key Findings

* Trust-Weighted Rating was higher than the Simple Average Rating.
* Products with large review volumes contributed more significantly to platform trust.
* High ratings alone did not always indicate product reliability.
* Products with high return rates received lower trust scores despite strong star ratings.

## Business Insights

Customer trust is influenced by both satisfaction and consistency. Products with strong ratings, large review counts, and lower return rates create higher purchase confidence and contribute positively to overall platform credibility.

## Conclusion

Trust-Weighted Rating provides a more realistic assessment of product quality than traditional rating metrics. E-commerce businesses can use this approach to improve product recommendations, promotional strategies, and customer trust management.
